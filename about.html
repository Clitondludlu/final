Custom Press Sa (pty) Ltd

A proudly registered South African business, dedicated to delivering high-quality printing and apparel solutions. We specialize in sublimation printing, vinyl transfers, and direct-to-film (DTF) printing, producing vibrant, long-lasting results for any project. Our services and products include: Custom prints for clothing, merchandise, and promotional items Blank apparel for businesses, brands, and resellers Printing equipment such as heat presses and vinyl cutters, including durable combo heat press machines. Whether you’re launching your own clothing line, creating branded gear, or expanding your printing capabilities, Custom Press SA offers the products, machinery, and expertise to help you succeed.
